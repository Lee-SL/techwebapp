Get Coaching

Career Coaching to get your first Tech Job!

Have you wondered how you can land your first tech job with all the competition thats out there? Maybe you are considering doing a masters or further study before you can land a job. Please reconsider. Employers are looking for skills more so than degrees. That's why you need a coach who's been through all that and will lead you along the right path!

What will career coaching do for you?

Find Your Strength

Find out together what you are good at and what you enjoy doing and come up with a career plan to make it appeal to employers!

Stand Out From the Crowd

There is currently a big disconnect from Uni to Work Coaching will help you gain the correct skills desired by employers!

Networking

Coach will help put you to the employers from their network and thus help you gain the upper hand in getting that interview and offer!

The Journey

Program is designed to be a 2 months journey. With three face to face sessions (each session is around 1hr).


Generally the sessions are spaced out during the 2 months however student may book more sessions if there is a need.


At the end of the 3 sessions the coach will then present the student to potential employers and with very high likelihood of landing an interview.

Try it Now

Whats included in the 3 sessions?

Session 1

Create personalised career plan, what do you want to do? What is the market demand? What should you focus on to stand out?

Session 2

Make sure you are still motivated and on the right track to hit career plan. Can help with technical blocker or career direction coaching.

Session 3

At this point your career plan should be nearly complete if not complete. Practise interview. Practise whiteboard review. Walkthrough your solutions.
